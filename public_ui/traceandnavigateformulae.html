The simple privacy policy

Thank you for using our simple add-on to make your life easier in google sheets!

We take your privacy very seriously and want to be transparent about how we collect and use your data.

Processing and Storage: Our add-on processes and analyzes the content of your spreadsheet, including formulae and values, to allow you to navigate through it. This information is analysed in google cloud, but we do not store any of this data on our servers. All of your data is stored on your machine.

Anonymous Usage Data Collection: We may collect anonymous usage data to improve our add-on's performance and features. This data is collected in an anonymized and aggregated form and does not contain any personal information or identifiable data.

Third-Party Services: We do not share any of your data with third-party services, and we do not use any third-party analytics tools.

Security: We take all reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, you should know that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to Privacy Policy: We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. If we make any material changes to the policy, we will notify users through the add-on or this page.

By using our Google Sheets Worksheet Add-On, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at mail@newinn.co.
